Hey! My name is Hannah Olson.

Since a very young age, I’ve always had a passion for creating artwork across various mediums. As digital art grew in popularity, I picked up a tablet and stepped foot into the world of digital drawing. After seeing the endless possibilities of digital art and design; I decided to level up my skills, get paid for my passion, and pursue an education in graphic deign. Since expanding my knowledge, my design portfolio has grown extensively and improved drastically. Over my journey of taking a condensed course, I learned to design efficiently and on tight timelines, without missing any details.

My design work is inclined to light-hearted, bubbly, expressive brand personalities. Through in-depth brand guides, to packaging design, to social media assets; I specialize in creating radiant and cohesive branding, true to it’s own core values.
